Overall Meaning

The lyrics to Peysoh's song "Peysoh" tell a story of a life of violence, gang activity, and loss. The lyrics talk about the necessity of putting in work and the danger of not being a killer. The singer also talks about the importance of being a real-life demon who is quick to retaliate. They present themselves as someone who has lost their father and their soul and are on a mission to seek revenge. The lyrics paint a clear picture of the culture of violence within certain communities and the mentality that exists within these groups.


The lyrics also contain elements of bravado and pride, with the singer boasting about their Glock and their ability to take down anyone who opposes them. They appear to have a strong sense of loyalty towards their friends, as evidenced by the line "Them my motherfuckin' friends," suggesting that they will go to great lengths to protect those they care about. The lyrics also express a disdain for those who snitch in jail, calling them out as being raised around "bitches."


Overall, the lyrics to Peysoh's song "Peysoh" offer a raw and unfiltered look into the life of someone who has experienced violence, loss, and hardship. The lyrics reflect a culture of violence and the desperate need to prove oneself as someone who is tough and capable of taking on anyone who opposes them.
