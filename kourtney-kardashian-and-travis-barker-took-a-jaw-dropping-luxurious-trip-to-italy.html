Kourtney Kardashian And Travis Barker Took A Jaw-Dropping Luxurious Trip To Italy

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker have heated things up in a big way this summer. Their excessive displays of PDA and ever-evolving romance have kept fans glued to social media to see what they do next, and their followers were pleasantly surprised to see the two embark on a romantic trip to Italy together. This wasn't just an ordinary trip, either. It was packed with romance and a very meaningful leap of faith on the part of Travis Barker. Travis was the lone survivor of a fatal plane crash that left him with third-degree burns over the majority of his body. Due to the intense trauma that he faced after the crash, he hadn't flown since that fateful day that his plane went down. Since dating Kourtney, he was able to overcome his fear, and the two embarked on a romantic international flight.

EOnline reports that these lovebirds certainly showed the world that they know how to celebrate this milestone moment and appreciate the love they share for one another. In true Kardashian style, they have embarked on some of the most spectacular adventures that Italy has to offer, and they've spared no expense.

Dolce & Gabbana Fashion Show

The Dolce & Gabbana Alta Moda Fashion Show attracted the "who's who" in the world of fashion, and of course, Kourtney and Travis scored front row seats alongside their family. They were able to catch the very best view of the supermodels strutting their stuff on the runway and looked completely at ease among the exclusive guests that attended this lavish event.

Of course, they made sure to look the part, too. Kourtney dropped big bucks on an ultra-sheer Dolce & Gabbana dress, while Travis boldly opted to wear a sleek, all-black suit without a dress shirt underneath. His chest and neck tattoos were his fashion accent, and the two cozied up to one another for the duration of this event.

Yacht Life

For most people, a yacht is quite out of reach, but Kourtney and Travis dropped a whole lot of money on a private, luxury yacht while vacationing in Italy, and they seemed to enjoy every single moment aboard their incredibly expensive vessel. Clearly smitten with another, they posed for a variety of selfies as they took in the sights of the clear skies and smooth waters. They enjoyed some of the most wondrous views of the Italian landscape and were able to fully appreciate the rich culture and incredible natural elements that surrounded them.

The yachting life included some elaborate snacks and a whole lot of sun-worshipping. Escaping to the open water together afforded Kourtney and Travis the luxury of isolation and freedom from the paparazzi, and they took full advantage of their time together, smooching on the deck and enjoying one another's company.

Touring Italy In A Gondola

Soaking up the luxurious lifestyle that Italy has to offer, Kourtney and Travis discovered the region from their very own private Gondola. They truly lived the life that millions of their adoring fans could only ever dream of, as their guided gondola showed them the sights of the picturesque Italian landscape, the city, and all of the incredible architecture that most people can only dream of experiencing. The relaxing, romantic afternoon was a refreshingly slow pace compared to the usual paparazzi frenzy that surrounds them. They were able to relax and unwind while they toured the space from the comfort of the slow-moving waters, posing to snap selfies to share with their fans.

Shopping And Dining

Being a Kardashian and dating a Kardashian equates to being able to indulge in all aspects of a vacation! Enjoying the most expensive food and drinks that Italy has to offer, Kourtney and Travis hit up the most prestigious hot spots in Portofino and indulged in the hearty Italian cuisine and, of course, the wine. They made sure to venture out for every meal and selected the top-end restaurants catered by the region's most talented chefs. Kourtney and Travis stunned the locals each time they stepped out. Truly proving to be a power couple in every sense of the word, they dressed to the nines each time they embarked on a restaurant adventure, and of course, they ordered the most elaborate menu items each time. Without any limits or financial hesitations, these lovebirds enjoyed the flavors of the region to the very fullest.

They held hands as they frolicked in the street and enjoyed some authentic gelato, popping in and out of shops with no limit to their spending and not a care in the world.

No stranger to haute-couture, Kourtney made some stops at some of the finest shops and was able to pick out some incredible outfits to take home with her. Surely soon to be shared with fans, her Italian threads.

Judging by the incredible time they had together while soaking up the rich Italian culture that surrounded them, it's fair to assume Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker will embark on another expensive vacation together very soon.
