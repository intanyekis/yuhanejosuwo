Tracey Bregman was married to Ron Recht in real life. Tracey and Ron got married in 1987 and divorced in 2010.

Likewise, the actress was seen on the big screen in 1981’s Happy Birthday to Me. The subsequent year, she acted as Elizabeth in The Concrete Jungle.

The actress returned to soap opera in 1983 with The Young and the Restless. She won the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Younger Actress in a Drama Series in 1985 of her performance as Lauren Fenmore.

The same character was reprised in The Bold and the Beautiful.
