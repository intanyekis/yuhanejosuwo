Who is Tom Brady?

Tom Brady is an American footballer who plays as a quarterback for the New England Patriots of the National Football League (NFL).

He is the only quarterback to win seven Super Bowls and the only player to win them all playing for one team.

He has also won four Super Bowl MVP Awards with the New England Patriots. Brady is a friend of former American President Donald Trump.

In 2021, Tom wins the seventh title as Tampa Bay Buccaneers beat Kansas City.

He recently announce his retirement from the NFL after 23 seasons on 1st February 2023.

Tom Brady: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood

Tom Brady was born Thomas Edward Patrick Brady Jr. on August 3, 1977, in San Mateo, California, United States. Tom holds an American nationality and he is of Mixed (Irish, Swedish, Norwegian, Polish) ethnicity.

He was born as the only son and fourth child of Galynn Patricia and Thomas Brady, Sr. His father owns a financial planning business.

Tom Brady has three elder sisters named Nancy, Julie, and Maureen. His father is of Irish descent and his mother is of Norwegian-Swedish and Polish ancestry.

In which school did Tom Brady attend?

Tom Brady attended Junípero Serra High School in San Mateo, California as a child and later attended the University of Michigan where he played football from 1995 to 1999.

During that time, he was an intern at Merrill Lynch Wealth Management. In 2003, he was inducted into the Junipero Serra High School Hall of Fame.

Tom Brady: Professional Life, Career

After graduation, Tom was selected by the professional American football team, New England Patriots in the sixth round of the 2000 NFL draft.

After playing as an extra for a few seasons, he became their starting quarterback. As a New England Patriots player, he has won two league MVP awards, first in 2007 and second in 2010.

Tom Brady successfully became one of only two players to win five Super Bowls and the only quarterback to win five Super Bowls. He became the only player to win five Super Bowls playing for one team.

He played with New England Patriots his entire life and he is one of the most successful American footballers of all time.

Brady has been honored with four Super Bowl MVP awards till now and he has been selected to 12 Pro Bowls. He has led his team to 14 titles, more than any other quarterback in NFL history.

In 2016, Tom Brady became the fourth all-time in career passing yards and third in career passer rating. Brady is considered one of the greatest quarterbacks in American football history.
