Moonbin best friend is the popular girl group GFRIEND’s member SinB. The fans of both dancers adore their chemistry and friendship a lot. 

Their playful bickering and inside jokes are heartwarming and delightful. The two have been seen sharing adorable moments on screen, and fans couldn’t get enough of their charisma and friendliness.

Moonbin’s and SinB’s friendship dates back to when they were kids. The two idols practically grew up together because they lived next door to each other.

Their chemistry stems from the fact that their careers have been so aligned. The two dancers started their journey toward becoming a star together. 

Their first gig was modeling for a children’s clothing brand. Pictures from that time show the two innocent kids looking the happiest with beaming smiles. 

Not only that, Moonbin and SinB even went to the same dance academy. 

SinB made her debut just a year before Moonbin. She debuted as a member of the South Korean girl group ‘GFRIEND.’

Whereas Moonbin made his debut in 2016 as part of the famous boy band ‘ASTRO.’
