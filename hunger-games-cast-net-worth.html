The Hunger Games: Which Actor Has Made It The Biggest, Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on July 31, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  959 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Hunger Games is arguably the most popular teen dystopian dramas out there, and it certainly proved so with its box office numbers and the popularity of all of these characters - specifically Katniss Everdeen. Of course, this franchise also shaped the careers of many of the young actors who participated in it.

From Liam Hemsworth to Donald Sutherland, there were plenty of new and old faces, but this flick undoubtedly helped them all in their career. It's been a handful of years, so, it's time to stack them up. Here's the cast of this hit franchise now, ranked by net worth (according to Celebrity Net Worth).

10 Willow Shields - $2 Million

This actress's first starring role was in this series, as Primrose Everdeen, and with that and more, she's made herself a $2 million net worth. She was on Dancing With the Stars in 2015, and most recently, she's starring in the series, Spinning Out.

She only has just over a dozen credits to her name, but she has two movies currently in production. Plus, for where this 20-year-old is at, her net worth is certainly impressive.

9 Amandla Stenberg - $2 Million

This young actress definitely made her claim to fame playing Rue in The Hunger Games, but right after, she had a recurring role in the series, Sleepy Hollow. This actress made waves as Rue, but her career is certainly far from over.

Most recently, she's starred in huge gits like The Darkest Minds and The Hate U Give. With another flick is production, this actress's career is only just beginning.

8 Josh Hutcherson - $20 Million

Josh Hutcherson was a heartthrob for all teens growing up, and he's been acting since he was a young child. Of course, his fame was solidified when he portrayed Peeta Mallark, and everyone couldn't help but fall in love with him.

However, unlike the others, Hutcherson's career actually slowed down, and most of his fame and fortune came from his earlier years. Obviously, he's just living his best life, and starring in some television series.

7 Stanley Tucci - $25 Million

It might be surprising that this acting legend has such a modest net worth, but he's also slid over to the television scene, and less box office hits He was perfect as Caesar Flickerman in The Hunger Games, though, and there's no one that could have done it better.

Fans should watch out for this actor, though, because he's hitting the big screen hard in the next couple of years with a handful of new movies. One of these includes the newest flick of another series, The King's Man.

6 Liam Hemsworth - $26 Million

Liam Hemsworth stole everyone's hearts when he starred alongside Miley Cyrus in The Last Song, but he proved his versatility and talent - and his handsomeness - when he hit the big screen as Gale Hawthorne.

With just a couple of dozen acting credits to his name, it's actually wildly impressive that his net worth is what it is. Fans hope he isn't going anywhere anytime soon.

5 Elizabeth Banks - $50 Million

Elizabeth Banks is the queen of bringing unique characters to life, and she certainly made Effie Trinket more memorable than anyone thought possible. This actress has a whopping amount of films and shows under her belt, and it's no surprise she has such a net worth.

Of course, she mainly participates in comedies and dramas, and this teen series was definitely one of the top box office hits for this talented and popular actress.

4 Lenny Kravitz - $50 Million

This musical legend can definitely attribute most of his impressive net worth to his huge success in the music industry, but movie fans were super happy to see him hit the big screen and bring Cinna to life.

While music is his game, he has dabbled in acting, but this is undoubtedly his biggest role in film. Of course, fans can listen to his beautiful voice in a ton of movie soundtracks.

3 Donald Sutherland - $60 Million

It's unlikely that anyone could have been as convincing as President Snow as this long-time actor. Sutherland is a legend in the industry, and with two Golden Globe wins, he has nearly 200 acting credits to his career!

There are many incredible flicks that have contributed to his net worth, and he's even had some roles in television series. Plus, he's not done yet, because he has a new series and two new movies coming out soon.

2 Woody Harrelson - $70 Million

Woody Harrelson might just be one of the most talented actors of today. With three Oscar nominations, this actor had long been slaying on the big screen before he joined the cast of this series as Haymitch Abernathy.

Surprisingly, this was one of his more serious roles, and it's certainly a memorable one for all kinds of movie fans. With $70 million to show for it, this man has had a stellar career, and it's far from over.

1 Jennifer Lawrence - $130 Million

Arguably, Lawrence was one of the least known on this list before The Hunger Games hit the big screen. She has already been recognized by the Academy, and she had participated in a few critically-acclaimed films.

However, no one could have guessed how much playing Katniss Everdeen would launch her career into one of the most popular in Hollywood. Lawrence has been at the top of everyone's list for years, and it's clear she has so much to offer. Her $130 million net worth is far from surprising.
