Prashant Kishor Early Life/Career

Early Life: - Prashant Kishor is from the Rohtas district's Koran village, Sasaram, but now lives in Bihar's Buxar. Kishor finished his secondary studies there. Kishor was reportedly hired as a political strategist for the BJP's pre-election campaign while working pro gratis and without holding any job in the BJP or Gujarat Government. On September 16, 2018, he was hired as the political strategist of the Janata Dal (United) political party.

Career: - In preparation for India's general election in May 2014, Kishor founded Citizens for Accountable Governance (CAG), a media and public relations firm. Kishor was credited with developing a creative marketing and advertising strategy for Narendra Modi, including the Chai pe Charcha talks, 3D rallies, the Run for Unity Manthan, and social media programmes.

Kishor was one of the most key persons in Modi's team pushing plans for months before the 2014 elections, according to Nilanjan Mukhopadhyay, author of 'Narendra Modi: The Man, The Times.'
