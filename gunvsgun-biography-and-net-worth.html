GunVsGun is a Canadian YouTube channel created and managed by the Canadian brothers; Tommy and Danny. On their channel, the duo upload videos about Nerf guns in the form of Nerf gun wars, reviews, twisted comedy, unboxing, and many more. They each have other channels named Terrible Tommy and Danny Gun. Tommy and Danny also run a second channel which is their vlog channel called Big Boy Toys.

The GunVsGun channel has over 5.7 million subscribers with an accumulated total view of over 2.1 billion. The channel averagely gets a daily total view of 500,000. There are no background details on the brothers, Tommy and Danny, however, they both stand at 5 feet 7 inches and weigh 60kg and 65kg respectively. The duo has dark hair with extremely dark eyes.
