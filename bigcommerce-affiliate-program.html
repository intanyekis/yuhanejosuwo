Pricing and Features of BigCommerce

BigCommerce offers a range of pricing plans designed to suit the needs of different businesses. Whether you’re just starting out or running a large e-commerce website, BigCommerce has a plan for you.

Essentials Plan

Starting at $29.95 per month, the Essentials plan provides essential features to help you build and grow your online storefront. Key features of this plan include:

  • Multi-currency support: Reach customers around the world with built-in multi-currency support.
  • Professional reporting tools: Gain insights into your business performance with advanced reporting tools.
  • Mobile-responsive website: Build a website that looks great on any device, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for your customers.

Plus Plan

The Plus plan, priced at $79.95 per month, includes all the features of the Essentials plan and additional advanced features to enhance your online store. Some of the key features of the Plus plan are:

  • Customer groups: Segment your customers and customize their shopping experiences based on their preferences.
  • Persistent cart: Allow customers to add items to their cart and preserve them across multiple sessions.
  • Stored credit cards: Enable customers to securely save their credit card information for faster checkout.

Pro Plan

The Pro plan is designed for larger e-commerce websites and is priced at $299.95 per month. In addition to the features of the Plus plan, the Pro plan offers advanced tools to optimize your online store. Some notable features of the Pro plan include:

  • Product filtering: Help customers find exactly what they’re looking for with customizable product filters.
  • Google Customer Reviews: Boost customer confidence by collecting and displaying genuine customer reviews on your website.

Enterprise Plans

For businesses with unique and specific requirements, BigCommerce offers customized Enterprise plans. These plans are tailored to meet your business’s specific needs, and the pricing is determined based on your requirements. Contact BigCommerce to discuss your Enterprise plan.

Now that you’re familiar with the pricing plans and features of BigCommerce, you can choose the plan that best suits your business and start building your online storefront with confidence.

How Much Can You Earn as a BigCommerce Affiliate?

The BigCommerce affiliate program offers a generous commission structure. As an affiliate, you have the opportunity to earn affiliate commissions and referral earnings, helping you monetize your website and increase your earnings. Here’s a breakdown of how the commission structure works:

Referral PlanAffiliate Commission
Standard Plans200% of the plan value
Enterprise Referrals$1,500 per referral

When a referral signs up for a standard plan, you can earn 200% of the plan value as your affiliate commission. For example, if a referral chooses the Plus plan priced at $79.95 per month, you will earn double that amount, meaning you’ll earn $159.90 for that referral.

In addition to the generous commissions on standard plans, you also have the opportunity to earn $1,500 for every Enterprise referral. This allows you to earn significant referral earnings when referring larger businesses to the BigCommerce platform.

It’s important to note that the commissions you earn are not recurring, but there is no limit to how much you can earn. The more referrals you bring in, the more you can potentially earn as a BigCommerce affiliate.

Payment Thresholds and Methods

Affiliates participating in the BigCommerce affiliate program have flexible payment options and a monthly payout frequency. BigCommerce ensures that affiliates can conveniently withdraw their earnings without any payment limit. The platform supports two popular payment methods:

  • PayPal: Affiliates can choose to receive their commissions directly into their PayPal accounts. This option offers a secure and hassle-free way to access earnings.
  • Direct Deposit: For added convenience, affiliates can opt for direct deposit, which allows commissions to be deposited directly into their bank accounts. This method eliminates the need for manual transactions and provides a seamless transfer of funds.

    • To maintain transparency and accuracy in the payment process, BigCommerce employs a monthly payout frequency. This means that affiliates receive their commissions once a month, ensuring a regular flow of income.

    Before commissions are dispersed, there is a waiting period of at least one month to allow for the verification of commissions by an account manager. This process ensures that commissions are accurately calculated and credited to affiliates’ accounts.

    By providing multiple payment options and a consistent monthly payout frequency, the BigCommerce affiliate program offers affiliates the convenience and financial stability they need to make the most of their referral earnings.

    Pros and Cons of the BigCommerce Affiliate Program

    As with any affiliate marketing program, the BigCommerce Affiliate Program has its own set of advantages and disadvantages that potential affiliates should consider. Here is a breakdown of the pros and cons of joining the program:

    Advantages of the BigCommerce Affiliate Program:

    • Brand Reputation: BigCommerce is a well-known and trusted name in the ecommerce industry. As an affiliate, you can leverage the brand’s reputation to boost credibility and increase conversions.
    • Generous Commission Rates: The BigCommerce Affiliate Program offers attractive commission rates, including a 200% bounty per referral’s plan price and $1,500 per enterprise customer. These high commissions can translate into significant earnings for affiliates.
    • No Obligations or Limits: Unlike some affiliate programs, the BigCommerce program does not impose any obligations or limits on the number of sales affiliates must make. This allows affiliates to work at their own pace and maximize their earning potential.
    • Comprehensive Tracking and Reporting: The program utilizes the Impact platform, which provides affiliates with comprehensive tracking and reporting capabilities. Affiliates can easily monitor their clicks, trials, sales, and commissions through a user-friendly affiliate dashboard.

    Disadvantages of the BigCommerce Affiliate Program:

    • Non-Recurring Commissions: It’s important to note that the commissions earned through the BigCommerce Affiliate Program are not recurring. While the initial referral commission can be substantial, affiliates will not earn additional commissions for subsequent purchases made by the same customer.
    • Limited Information on Cookie Length: The exact details about the cookie length in the BigCommerce Affiliate Program are not readily available. Affiliates may need to reach out to the program’s support team for more information on how long their referrals’ cookies remain active.

    It’s important to weigh these pros and cons before joining the BigCommerce Affiliate Program. Consider your own goals, capabilities, and preferences to determine if the program aligns with your affiliate marketing strategy.
