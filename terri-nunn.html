    Terri Nunn Career and Star Wars

    Nunn auditioned for the role of Princess Lela in Star Wars in 1976. Unfortunately. the role went to Carrie Fisher. Nunn also posed for nude photos in the February issue of Penthouse in 1977 where she used the name, Betsy Harris. She has denied that the photos here hers because the photos were taken when she was 13 years old.

    In 1978, she joined Berlin and temporarily left the group the following year with an aim to pursue her acting career. She has acted in several Tv shows including T.J Hooker, Lou Grant, and James at 15.

    Nunn rejoined Berlin in 1981 as the lead vocalist. Working with Berlin, her greatest success was the top-selling 1986 single “Take My Breath Away”, the theme song for the film Top Gun. She is also known for other songs like “Sex (I’m A…)”, “The Metro”, “You Don’t Know”, “No More Words” and “Masquerade”.

    Nunn dated Michael Hutchence in 1985 for a short period of time. She met Michael at an INXS concert in London while Berlin was recording the album Count Three & Pray. Unfortunately, their relationship ended when INXS returned to Australia.

    Nunn left Berlin in 1985 and recorded the song “Dancing in Isolation” for the film Better Off Dead. She also performed a duet in 1989 with Paul Carrack. The duet was called Romance which was included on the soundtrack to the film Sing.

    Nunn recorded and released a solo album called Moment of Truth in association with David Z, Prince’s Paisley Park producer in 1991. She also approached lead singer Andrew Eldritch of Sisters of Mercy with a song idea and collaborated with him on “Under the Gun” in 1993.

    In 1996, Nunn obtained usage rights to the band name “Berlin.” She reformed that band with new members who began recording and touring. She released two EPs Fall into Heaven and Fall Into Heaven in 1999.

    Berlin’s original lineup reunited for the VH1 series “Bands Reunited” in 2003. Nonetheless, she contributed sex and dating advice to Carrie Borzillo-Vrenna’s book Cherry Bomb.

    On the 2009 Regeneration Tour, she sang across the United States. It also featured Martin Fry of ABC, Wang Chung, and Cutting Crew.

    Nunn appeared as a guest judge on season 2 of RuPaul’s Drag Race in 2010 where she mentored the contestants in a rock-themed singing challenge. Nunn co-hosts Between the Sheets. Betewwe the Sheets is a popular podcast on Webcast media station Hottalkla.com.
