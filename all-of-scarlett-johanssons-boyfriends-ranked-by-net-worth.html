Scarlett Johansson is a name synonymous with beauty, talent, and success. She has captured the hearts of millions with her incredible acting skills and charismatic screen presence. Apart from being a successful actress, her high-profile love life is constantly in the limelight.

Over the years, Scarlett Johansson has linked herself to several high-profile men in Hollywood, each with an impressive net worth. Starting from Jared Leto to Ryan Reynolds, Scarlett Johansson’s dating history is star-studded.

Nate Naylor - $1.2 Million

Nate Naylor is a New York-based advertising executive romantically involved with Scarlett Johansson from 2011 to 2012. Although their relationship was relatively short-lived, it garnered significant media attention. There isn’t much information about Naylor’s net worth, but it is rumored to be around $1.2 Million. Naylor has a successful advertising career and has worked with several high-profile clients.

Romain Dauriac - $1.5 Million

French journalist Romain Dauriac captured Scarlett's heart in 2012, and the couple tied the knot in 2014, as per reports by The US Sun. They share a daughter, Rose Dorothy Dauriac, born in 2014. Despite their seemingly idyllic life, the couple decided to part ways in 2017. Romain Dauriac's net worth is $1.5 million, placing him just above Nate Naylor. Dauriac's career as a journalist and art curator has contributed to his net worth, and he continues to be involved in art.

Colin Jost - $10 Million

Scarlett Johansson's current husband, Colin Jost, is a successful comedian, writer, and actor best known for his work on Saturday Night Live. The couple began dating in 2017 and got engaged in 2019. They exchanged vows in an intimate ceremony in 2020. Colin has accumulated an impressive $10 Million net worth from his phenomenal TV career. His career has been on the rise, with his work on SNL earning him multiple Emmy nominations and his recent foray into film with roles in movies like Tom and Jerry and How to Be Single.

Jack Antonoff - $25 Million

Jack Antonoff is a musician and producer known for his work with famous artists like Taylor Swift and Lorde. He is one of Scarlett Johansson’s earliest love interests, and the duo dated during their high school years. Although their teenage romance was brief, it does give a trip down the memory lane. Antonoff boasts an impressive net worth of $25 million. He has had a successful career as a musician, both as a member of the band Bleachers and as a producer for some of the biggest names in the music industry.

Josh Hartnett - $25 Million

Josh Hartnett is an actor known for his roles in films like Pearl Harbor and Black Hawk Down. He had a high-profile relationship with Scarlett Johansson from 2005 to 2007. Their romance made headlines, but ultimately, the couple decided to go their separate ways. Hartnett has a net worth of $25 million and shares the fourth spot with Jack Antonoff. Hartnett's career is infused with critical and commercial success, and he continues to act in film and television projects.

Sean Penn - $70 Million

Academy Award-winning actor Sean Penn and Scarlett Johansson were rumored to be dating in 2011 following her divorce from Ryan Reynolds. Their relationship generated significant buzz in the media, although it was never officially confirmed. Penn has a stellar $70 Million net worth, as per Wealthy Gorilla, making him one of the richest Johansson has ever dated. His illustrious career has spanned decades, and he has earned numerous accolades for his work in films like Mystic River and Milk.

Jared Leto - $90 Million

Actor and musician Jared Leto is known for his roles in films like Requiem for a Dream, Suicide Squad, and Dallas Buyers Club. He was briefly linked to Scarlett Johansson in 2004, as reported by US Weekly. The pair sparked dating rumors after being spotted together on several occasions. Leto has accumulated an astounding $90 Million net worth through his critically acclaimed career, making him the second richest on this list. Leto's career has been marked by critical acclaim and commercial success, with his work as the lead singer of the band Thirty Seconds to Mars and his Oscar-winning performance in Dallas Buyers Club.

Ryan Reynolds - $350 Million

Scarlett Johansson's first husband, actor Ryan Reynolds, tops this list with a staggering net worth of $350 million. The couple began dating in 2007, got engaged in 2008, and married later that year, as per Pop Sugar. Unfortunately, their marriage ended in divorce in 2011. Reynolds has since remarried and shares three children with his wife, actress Blake Lively. Reynolds' career has skyrocketed in recent years, with his role as Deadpool catapulting him to superstardom and significantly contributing to his net worth.

Scarlett Johansson's love life is as diverse and fascinating as her acting career. Her romantic partners have boasted impressive net worths, from high school sweethearts to Hollywood A-listers. Scarlett's personal life remains a topic of interest for fans and the media as she continues to captivate audiences worldwide.
