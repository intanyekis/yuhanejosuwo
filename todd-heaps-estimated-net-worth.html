Todd Benjamin Heap’s Background and Career

Todd Benjamin Heap, a retired American Football tight end, was born on March 16, 1980, in Mesa, Arizona USA. Over a 12-season career in the National Football League (NFL), he played for the Baltimore Ravens (2001-2010) and Arizona Cardinals (2011-2012), earning two Pro-Bowl selections in 2002 and 2003 and making the All-Pro team in 2003.

As of mid-2017, Todd Heap’s net worth is estimated to be around $13 million, primarily from his successful football career spanning from 2001 to 2012.
