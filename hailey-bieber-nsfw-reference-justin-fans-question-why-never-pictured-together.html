Hailey Bieber Makes NSFW Reference To Justin As Fans Question Why Theyre Never Pictured Together

 Posted on May 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  647 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Hailey Bieber is refusing to directly respond to the ongoing speculation about her marriage. Still, she seemed to make a subtle reference to her husband, Justin Bieber, and it was a bit spicy.

Posting to her Instagram, Hailey shared a slideshow of snaps from her recent adventures, capturing the photo dump “Spring Things.” The first photo showed a series of yellow and pink flowers against a green backdrop, while the second was a fierce photo of Hailey in a blazer.

Why Fans Find Hailey’s Latest Instagram Post To Be Suspicious

Other photos included an image of Hailey’s dog and some more snaps of the model showing off her recent outfits. But it was one of the middle photos that caught the attention of fans. It was a close-up of a sign that said, “Not for sex lol.” Moreover, the final slide int he post was a video of two frogs on top of each other with the writing, "Us in another universe."

Many of the post’s comments question where Justin was and whether Hailey was trying to send a hidden message, The U.S. Sun reports. Some question whether she was suggesting they're no longer together or struggling with their intimate life.

"Your husband just lost a friend and you go and post pictures of yourself???" one person wrote, while another added, "How about a picture of your man? This is not how it works."

While Hailey’s post may have seemed inconspicuous, some fans believe the sign was an indirect message for followers who’re demanding to see photos of Hailey and Justin together, since fans are noticing it’s been a while since the couple posted anything together online.

Why Fans Think Hailey And Justin Are Living Separately

Hailey and Justin have made a few public appearances together in recent weeks despite the divorce rumors, including at Coachella. The couple shared a lovey-dovey moment in the crowd while enjoying music. Fellow concertgoers captured the moment, which went viral on social media.

However, one of Hailey’s music festival outfits seemed to further the relationship problem rumors. The model was pictured backstage at Coachella in an oversized outfit, which is uncharacteristic of her style. While some people accused her of simply wanting to copy Justin’s infamously unkempt look, others said she may have been wearing something baggy in hopes of starting pregnancy speculation to deflect from the divorce accusations.

"Now her fans going to say 'aww she’s wearing Justin’s baggy clothes, to cover her bump,'" one person wrote on a Reddit thread discussing Hailey’s Coachella look, while another said, "Clearly not pregnant- her stomach looks totally flat!?"

There continue to be rumors suggesting the couple are living separately, which was only fuelled when Justin purchased a $16 million mansion earlier this month. The singer was also spotted leaving a hotel, leading some fans to question if he’s been staying there instead of his home with Hailey.

It also didn’t help last month when Stephen Baldwin, Hailey’s father, made an Instagram post asking fans to pray for the couple without any context. This prompted Hailey to go online shortly after to encourage her followers not to believe the rumors about her marriage.

"Just FYI the stories and constant ‘blind items’ I see on TikTok are 100% of the time wrong. Made out of thin air… come from the land of delusion,” she wrote. "So I know it may be fun feeding into these stories but just know they’re always false xx sorry to spoil it."

Justin and Hailey have been married since 2018.
