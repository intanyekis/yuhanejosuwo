Mark Simone is an American radio personality that can be heard on WOR in New York City, New York, on weekdays from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m., in the morning and afternoon. In addition, he and New York Post writer Linda Stasi co-host the weekly What a Week on NY1 segment, which takes a lighthearted look at the news of the week.
