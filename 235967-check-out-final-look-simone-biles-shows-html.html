Check Out the Final Look Simone Biles Revealed of the Lavish Pool That She Built in Her Home

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  542 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The American gymnast showed off her newly-completed swimming pool via her Instagram stories. The final look of the long-anticipated project was a breathtaking sight to behold.

It is no news that artistic gymnast Simone Biles has other interests other than her dedication to the gymnastics world. Topping the list of her favorite downtime activities is her love for the poolside.

The 23-year-old gymnast showed off the final look of the new gigantic pool she built in her backyard, and fans could not have enough of the beautiful monument.

Simone Biles during the FIG Artistic Gymnastics World Championships on October 08, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

Biles' Instagram stories displayed the outlook of the pool from different angles. Two artistic seats positioned strategically at the side of the pool made it more glamorous.

In a close-up shot, the clear blue waters which blended into the overall scenery, accentuated the beauty of Biles' house, visibly a few feet away.

America's most decorated gymnast commenced the set-up of the leisure structure earlier this year. She kept her fans updated on the structure's progress even while it was in the works.

The star shared a revealing video of the then-uncompleted project in September, showing off all its breathtaking inclusions. The in-ground structure featured a hot tub, an extravagant tile arrangement, and an awning.

Biles went public with her relationship status in August.

A shot of Simone Biles' gorgeous pool posted on her Instagram story | Photo: Instagram/simonebiles

She also explained her intentions towards the pool, saying it was her way of beating the Houston heat. Fans of the 23-year-old are aware of her love for swimming pools, as she continually floods her Instagram feed with photos of herself enjoying poolside comfort, often with "no worries."

In addition to showcasing her poolside moments, Biles also puts her fit physique on display in jaw-dropping outfits. In one such post, the Olympic medalist sent fans raving with a photo of herself seated on the edge of a pool in a red two-piece swimsuit.

Another shot of the magnificent swimming pool on Instagram | Photo: Instagram/simonebiles

Amid her daunting career as a professional gymnast, many wonder how the star manages to make out time for her enjoyable moments in the pool while being an Olympic champion and looking the part. In a 2018 interview, Biles opened up about the effect of the tasking ordeal on her personal life.She said:

"It's hard mentally and physically. I remember some of the days, I'm like: 'Why am I here? What am I doing?' What did I do? But in the end, it's all worth it."

Notwithstanding, the 23-year-old has, through the years, maintained an interesting personal life with a sprinkle of romance. As most may recall, Biles went public with her relationship status in August.

She unveiled her new beau to the world via pictures shared on social media. The post officially confirmed the icon's relationship with NFL player Jonathan Owens.

Before hooking up with Owens, the gymnast sustained a long term relationship with fellow gymnast Stacey Ervin Jr. The duo parted ways in March after dating for three years.

With her successful career, a blossoming romance, an extravagant mansion, and a dream pool at just 23, Biles is undoubtedly living the dream.
